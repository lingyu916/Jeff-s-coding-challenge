Spaghetti Bolognese

The image resource

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red bean paste
  • ¼ cup golden syrup
  • ½ teaspoon alkaline water (lye water)
  • 2 tablespoons of melted shortening
  • ¾ cup cake flour
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten with 1 tablespoon of water

Instructions

  1. Fry the onion and minced beef until brown.
  2. Add the tinned tomatoes, tomato puree and salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Simmer for 20 minutes.

  4. Boil the spaghetti for 10 minutes.

  5. Serve meat sauce with the spaghetti.
